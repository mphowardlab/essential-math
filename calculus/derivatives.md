# Derivatives
$$
\begin{array}{lll}
\text{Rule} & f(x) & f'(x) \\
\hline
\text{Constant} & k & 0 \\
\text{Power} & x^n & nx^{n-1} \\
\text{Multiple} & k u(x) & k u'(x) \\
\text{Sum} & u(x) + v(x) & u'(x) + v'(x) \\
\text{Difference} & u(x) - v(x) & u'(x) - v'(x) \\
\text{Exponential} & a^x & \frac{a^x}{\ln a} \\
\text{Logarithm} & \log_a x & \frac{1}{x \ln a} \\
\end{array}
$$
