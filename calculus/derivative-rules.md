# Product, quotient, and chain rule

5 Examples:

````{example}

\begin{align}
(1) \quad f(x) &= 3\cos{x}+\sin{x}\\
f^{'}(x) &= 3\frac{d}{dx}(\cos{x})+4\frac{d}{dx}(\sin{x})\\
&= -3\sin{x} + 4\cos{x}\\
\end{align}
````
````{example}
\begin{align}
(2) \quad f(x) &= 2\sin{x}\cos{x}\\
u &= \sin{x} \quad v = \cos{x}\\
u^{'} &= \cos{x} \quad v^{'} = -\sin{x}\\
&= 2[\sin{x}(-\sin{x})+\cos{x}(\cos{x})]\\
\end{align}
````
