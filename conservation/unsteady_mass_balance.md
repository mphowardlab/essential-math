# **Mass Balance**

$$
\frac{dm}{dt} = \dot{m}_{in} - \dot{m}_{out}
$$

Since density is constant:

$$
\frac{d(\rho V)}{dt} = \rho \frac{dV}{dt} = 0
$$

Thus:

$$
\rho \frac{dV}{dt} = 0 \quad \Rightarrow \quad \frac{dV}{dt} = 0
$$
